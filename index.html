Privacy Policy for Mealie

Effective Date: 16 Sept 2025

Last Updated: 16 Sept 2025

Quick Summary: Mealie is your personal meal planning app. We only collect the information you provide to make the app work better for you, and we never sell your data to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Account Information: Email address and name when you create an account
  • Profile Data: Dietary preferences, food allergies, and household size
  • Recipe Data: Recipes you create, save, or organize into collections
  • Meal Plans: Your meal planning data and shopping lists

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app to improve functionality
  • Device Information: Basic device info to ensure app compatibility

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Mealie app services
  • Generate personalized meal plans based on your preferences
  • Create smart shopping lists from your meal plans
  • Sync your data across your devices
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Send important account and service updates

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted partners like Google Firebase who help us operate the app
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition

4. Third-Party Services

Mealie uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database, and app analytics
    Google Privacy Policy
  • Meal Plan Generation API: For AI-powered meal planning recommendations

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • User authentication through secure Firebase systems
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to personal data by our team

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View the personal data we have about you
  • Update: Modify your account information and preferences
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export: Download your recipes and meal planning data

You have the right to request complete deletion of your Mealie account and all associated personal data. This includes:

  • Your account information (name, email)
  • All saved recipes and recipe collections
  • Meal plans and shopping lists
  • Dietary preferences and allergy information
  • Any usage data associated with your account

To request account deletion:

  1. Send an email to: sudogonnie@gmail.com
  2. Include "Mealie Account Deletion Request" in the subject line
  3. Provide your registered email address for verification

What happens after you request deletion:

  • We will confirm your identity and process your request within 7 business days
  • All your personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete
  • This action is irreversible - you will lose access to all your recipes and meal plans

Alternative: You can also ask for only part of your data to be deleted.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account (either through the app or by request), we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we're required to retain it by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Mealie is designed for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date above.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, data deletion requests, or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: sudogonnie@gmail.com
  • Subject Line for Data Requests: "Mealie Data Request"

We'll respond to your inquiry within 7 business days.
